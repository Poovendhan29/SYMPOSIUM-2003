    1.D E B U G G I N G:-

   .Debugging in computer programming and engineering, is a multistep process that involves identifying a problem, 
    isolating the source of the problem, and then either correcting the problem or determining a way to work around it. 
    The final step of debugging is to test the correction or workaround and make sure it works.
